## Overview

Group and structure your documentation for easy access.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new folder">
    Go to the dashboard and click "New Folder". Name your folder by project or topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation">
    Within a folder, click "New Doc" or drag and drop files to upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorganize as needed">
    Drag folders or docs to reorder. Use tags for fast filtering later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
