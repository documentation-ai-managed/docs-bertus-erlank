## Overview

Bertus Erlank provides a centralized space to organize, manage, and collaborate on your project documentation. You create structured pages, embed code examples, and maintain version history effortlessly. This platform supports Markdown, MDX components, and custom workflows tailored for developers and project managers.

With Bertus Erlank, you streamline knowledge sharing across teams. Access searchable docs, integrate with GitHub, and export to various formats. Start building your documentation hub today.

## Quick Start

Get started with Bertus Erlank in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://bertus-erlank.example.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Click "New Workspace" and name it for your project, e.g., "MyApp Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Create `introduction.mdx` and add frontmatter:

    ```yaml
    ---
    title: My Project
    description: Overview of the project.
    ---
    ```

    Then add content starting with `## Overview`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Save and publish. Share the live URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
